South Loggia - 2nd Year

I dropped a cinnamon sugar croissant on the dirty loggia floor.

Because I was up until four in the morning doing work, I was sleep deprived enough to consider eating it. It was supposed to be my reward for getting a lot done. I didn't want a slight health inconvenience to ruin that.

Second year was proving to be a lot harder than the previous. I was slouching so much that I felt like I was slowly being dragged to hell.
